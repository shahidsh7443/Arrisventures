Few major sectors that we are serving in Non IT industries are Retail, Engineering, Construction, Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology etc.

The company operates via a team of experienced and dynamic professionals disciplined towards the process of high-end recruitment techniques. We are always ready to take up challenges that each assignment brings in by staying in course and committing our valuable resources until our client’s requirements are successfully fulfilled.

Our recruitment services for board-level executives are based on identifying and nurturing high-potential managers to help them become industry leaders of tomorrow. ArrisTechnologies employ some of the most advanced and contemporary sourcing strategies while banking upon our strong relationships within numerous industries to find the right talent for leadership and management positions.

Our team of leading global professionals brings a truly unique understanding of our client needs most thoroughly. After we are done with substantive talks with clients, we get the job specifics and parameters of the proposed position finally, getting a thorough understanding of the salary ranges and benefits which are clearly conveyed to the prospective candidate.

ArrisTechnoligies recruiters have extensive training and experience which allows them to configure the best solution with the right background, aptitude and approach.

ArrisTrraining offers latest and the most innovative and comprehensive training solutions to corporate organizations across India. With the perfect combination of effective hands-on training, excellent customer service and dynamic real-world learning, ArissTrraining helps IT and Non-IT company employees to discover a new level of performance within their professional training need.

We offer customized courses in personality development, behavioral assessments, communication and other soft skills along with technological skills training for IT Industry. We have top-notch trainers, facilitators and practicing specialists on board and / or as consultants who are expert in their specific fields. Along with our management, our trainers bring many years of practical experience and robust educational background to the table.

Our team of planners and strategists are well aware that the key to a successful training program rests on clearly grasping the customer requirement and outcomes for learning. We do not force a ready-to-use training program on our clients but rather take the time to understand the industry, the objectives and the level of reception from the end-users and create tailor-made training solutions for the same. Hence, no training program is similar to any other program we deliver.

ArrisTrraining spends enormous time to analyze client requirements through our unique training-needs analysis, where we figure out who needs training and what of nature. We follow this up with outlining clear and precise learning goals, a channel for clear feedback, measurement / assessment procedures and regular interaction with all stakeholders.

With expanding training budgets and an evolving HR department, addressing an organization's need for a knowledgeable, well-informed and up-to-date workforce is a challenging task. Working hand-in-hand with company’s top management to create a truly unique program, Arris’s training solutions help maintain and nurture an organization's relevance, adaptability, progressiveness and profitability.
